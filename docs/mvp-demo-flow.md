# MVP Demo Flow

## Objective

This document defines how GeoWorkbench should be demonstrated to geologists, technical teams, and potential industry partners.

The goal is to demonstrate:

- workflow usefulness
- geological understanding
- AI-assisted productivity
- practical usability

—not abstract AI claims.

---

# Recommended Demo Narrative

## Step 1 — Geological Project Dashboard

Show:

- active projects
- borehole counts
- AI alerts
- recent interpretations
- project summaries

Key message:

> GeoWorkbench centralizes geological workflows.

---

## Step 2 — Import Geological Data

Show:

- CSV/Excel/LAS upload
- auto-detected fields
- validation checks
- naming normalization

Key message:

> Geological data cleanup and ingestion becomes significantly easier.

---

## Step 3 — Borehole Interpretation Workspace

Show:

- lithology intervals
- geophysical logs
- seam visualization
- AI lithology suggestions
- editable interpretations

Key message:

> AI assists geologists while keeping them in control.

---

## Step 4 — Seam Correlation Workspace

Show:

- multiple boreholes
- seam continuity visualization
- AI correlation suggestions
- anomaly detection

Key message:

> Geological reasoning becomes faster and more consistent.

---

## Step 5 — AI Geological Assistant

Example questions:

- Which boreholes contain Seam-A?
- Show intervals with high resistivity.
- Summarize geological observations.
- Generate borehole report.

Key message:

> Geological knowledge becomes searchable and operational.

---

## Step 6 — AI Report Generator

Show:

- automatic geological summaries
- editable AI draft reports
- export workflows

Key message:

> Reporting effort can be dramatically reduced.

---

# Important Messaging

Avoid saying:

- AI replaces geologists
- fully automated geology
- autonomous interpretation

Prefer:

- AI-assisted interpretation
- workflow acceleration
- geological productivity
- human-in-the-loop intelligence

---

# Demo Success Criteria

The demo succeeds if geologists say:

- this reflects real workflows
- this could save time
- this reduces repetitive work
- this improves data organization
- this could become operationally useful
